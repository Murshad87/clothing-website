# Al Malka Garments & Boutique Center 👗🧵🎒💄

Welcome to the official repository for **Al Malka Garments & Boutique Center**, a clothing and boutique business based in Malka, Pakistan.  
This project is the source code for our website, which highlights our collections and helps customers connect with us easily.  

---

## 🌟 About Us
Al Malka Garments & Boutique Center offers a wide range of quality products, including:  
- 👕 Kidswear  
- 👔 Menswear  
- 👗 Womenswear  
- 🎓 School uniforms  
- 🎒 School bags  
- 💄 Cosmetics  

We are focused on **style, comfort, affordability, and durability** for all our customers.

## ✨ Features
- ✅ Modern and responsive website design
